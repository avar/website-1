ack community

Slack

beyondgrep.com has a dedicated Slack at https://beyondgrep.slack.com/. There you'll find an #ack channel.

The channel is currently invitation-only, so email andy@petdance.com to get an invitation sent to you. Soon we'll have it set up to the general public.

Mailing lists

There are three mailing lists for keeping up on the world of ack.

ack-users

Main list for discussion of ack for both ack's users and those looking to help contribute. New fans of ack may want to browse the archive for cool tricks.

https://groups.google.com/d/forum/ack-users

ack-announce

Very low volume list for announcements of new ack releases.

https://groups.google.com/d/forum/ack-announce

ack-dev

List for discussion of Ack's development - intended primarily for its developers. This is in addition to the ack2 GitHub project.

https://groups.google.com/d/forum/ack-dev
